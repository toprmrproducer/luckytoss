How LuckyToss works

Coin flip

A real 3D animated coin with hand-drawn heads and tails, live tallies, and a streak counter.

Standard dice

Roll 1 to 6 six-sided dice with true pip faces, a running total, and recent-roll history.

Polyhedral dice

Tabletop d4 to d20 with quantity and a +/- modifier, in classic dice notation like 3d8+2.

Every roll and flip happens locally in your browser using Math.random() — nothing you do here is ever sent to a server.
