About LuckyToss

LuckyToss is a simple, fast tool for flipping an animated 3D coin, rolling standard six-sided dice, or rolling a full set of polyhedral tabletop dice (d4 through d20) with a modifier — entirely inside your web browser. Every result comes fromMath.random(), so outcomes are fair and unbiased, and nothing is ever sent to a server.

Because there is no server-side processing, LuckyToss can stay completely free with no account, no signup, and no limits. The site is supported by on-page advertising.

LuckyToss tracks your streaks, tallies, and a short recent history for the coin flip and both dice rollers, so you can see patterns across a session without anything being saved once you close the tab.

Every roll and flip happens locally in your browser using Math.random() — nothing you do here is ever sent to a server.
