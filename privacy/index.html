Privacy Policy

LuckyToss runs entirely on your own device, inside your browser. No roll, flip, streak, or history is ever uploaded to, or stored on, any server operated by LuckyToss. Closing or refreshing the tab clears all session data.

LuckyToss uses standard web analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, referrers) and may display advertising through Google AdSense, which can set its own cookies subject to Google's own privacy policy.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the details on the About page.

Every roll and flip happens locally in your browser using Math.random() — nothing you do here is ever sent to a server.
